About Us

  • minecraft plugins - Our plugins are of very good quality with unique functions.
  • Discord bots - Our bots are very good and customizable.
  • Maps building - Our maps are very professional and beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your payments secure?

Yes, our payments are totally secure because we sell our products on the builtbybit.com website and there the payments are totally secure.

How can I get some of your products?

To purchase some of our products you have to visit our official builtbybit website Profile. and there you will find all our products available at the moment.

Where can I find your official support?

To join our official discord you can enter from the link where it says discord in the menu above or you can enter from this link Discord.gg/sstudios there you will find all the necessary information about our products or support and more information.

How can I join the SSTUDIOS team?

To join our studio team you need to know one of three things: programming, graphic design, construction, or if you are just looking to moderate you need to know the basics of discord moderation.

What benefits do we gain from your support for being a sstudios customer?

In our discord you get more priority in the support tickets you also get a role called customer that is only for those who buy products of sstudios you also get discounts on our paid products.
